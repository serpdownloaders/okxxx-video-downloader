## Troubleshooting

**The extension does not detect any video on the page**
Make sure you are on a valid `/video/<id>/` page and that video playback has started. The extension needs to see the iframe handoff before it can detect streams.

**The download fails or produces a corrupted file**
Try refreshing the page and restarting playback before attempting the download again. Network interruptions during the download process can cause incomplete files.

**Only one quality option is available**
Some videos on OK.xxx may only have a single stream version available. The extension will show whatever options the platform provides through the iframe handoff.

**The extension icon is grayed out on OK.xxx pages**
Verify that you are on a supported domain (ok.xxx, www.ok.xxx, or xiaoshenke.net) and that the page follows the `/video/<id>/` pattern.

**Downloads are slow or timing out**
Large video files can take time to download, especially on slower connections. Try downloading during off-peak hours or check your internet speed.
